Gem State Robotics Alliance

Empowering youth through robotics and STEM education

About Us

Gem State Robotics Alliance is the official FTC and FLL partner organization in Idaho. We promote robotics and STEAM education by supporting local teams and events.

Our Programs

We offer support for FIRST Tech Challenge and FIRST LEGO League programs, helping youth build technical and teamwork skills through robotics.

Check out our calendar for exciting competitions, community expos, and training workshops across the state.
