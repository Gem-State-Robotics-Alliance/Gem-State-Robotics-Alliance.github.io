Our Mission

Gem State Robotics Alliance is dedicated to expanding access to high-quality STEM education across Idaho by supporting robotics teams, competitions, and community outreach.

Our Programs

GSRA provides support to hands-on STEM education through competitive robotics programs that inspire students to think critically, work collaboratively, and solve real-world problems.
