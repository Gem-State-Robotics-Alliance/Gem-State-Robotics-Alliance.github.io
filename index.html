About Us

Gem State Robotics Alliance is commited to supporting FIRST teams in Idaho. We promote robotics and STEAM education by supporting local teams and events.

We offer support for FIRST Tech Challenge FIRST LEGO League and FIRST Robotics Competition, helping youth build technical and teamwork skills through robotics.
