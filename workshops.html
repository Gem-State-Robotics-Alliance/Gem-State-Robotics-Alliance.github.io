Workshops

Hands-on learning opportunities for students and mentors

Workshop 1: Robotics Basics

Learn the fundamentals of FTC and FLL robotics. Perfect for new teams or students wanting to sharpen their skills.

Workshop 2: Programming & Sensors

Explore coding strategies, sensor integration, and autonomous routines to take your robot to the next level.

Workshop 3: Competition Prep

Prepare for local and regional competitions with tips on strategy, robot inspection, and teamwork skills.
